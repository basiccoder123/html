Welcome to Bready's Bakery GH

Bredy's Bakery is a bakery that specializes in the production of bread, cakes, pastries, and other confectioneries. We are located at the heart of Accra, Ghana. We are open from 6:00 am to 8:00 pm from Monday to Saturday. We are closed on Sundays. We offer delivery services to our customers. We also offer catering services for events such as weddings, birthdays, and corporate events. Our products are made with the finest ingredients and are baked fresh daily. Visit us today and experience the taste of Bready's Bakery.
