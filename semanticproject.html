Horses

Horses are my favourite animal. Horses are magnificent creatures with a long history of companionship with humans.
Their powerful build, graceful movements, and intelligent nature have captivated people for centuries.
These herbivores are social animals, thriving in herds with complex social structures.
Horses possess a keen sense of hearing and sight, allowing them to navigate their surroundings and communicate with each other effectively.
They are known for their ability to learn and bond with humans, making them ideal partners for riding, racing, and other equestrian activities.
